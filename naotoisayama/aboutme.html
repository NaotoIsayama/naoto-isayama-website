About Me

I'm Naoto (Jacob) Isayama - a web designer and developer based in Edmonton.

I create distinctive, custom websites for small business that want to stand out - without compromising on usability or long-term manageability.

My process begins with in-depth research into your field, followed by a fully custom design tailored to you. Once we're aligned on the visual direction, I personally hand-code the website to ensure performance, accessibility, and attention to detail.

Every site I build includes a user-friendly content management solution, and I take the time to walk you through how everything works - so you're confident and in control from day 1.
